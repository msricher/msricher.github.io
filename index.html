About

I'm a PhD candidate with Ayers group at McMaster University, specializing in quantum chemistry and computational chemistry. My research is focused on the development of novel methods for computing the electronic structure of open shell and strongly correlated molecular systems, with a better trade-off between computational cost and accuracy than traditional methods. As part of the QC-Devs community, I contribute to several free and open-source quantum chemistry software projects, including ChemTools, IOData, and Procrustes.
